ABOUT US

Our aim is to raise public awareness of environmental issues, to encourage more ecologically-responsible behavior, and to educate people about the importance of understanding of environment's vulnerability and protecting it.

Our group consists of four members; Min Kaung Htet, Sint Sint Linn Lae, Kyal Sin Lin Lint Aung, and Hkaw Seng Grin.

Kyal Sin Lin Lint Aung

Creator
My name is Kyal Sin Lin Lint Aung. I am from Year 8 Helio EDUSN Digital school. I've always been willing to promote perception among ordinary people about environmental issues and help climate change to make the better world as much as I can.

Sint Sint Linn Lae

Co-Creator
My name is Sint Sint Linn Lae. I am from Year 8 Helio EDUSN Digital School. I always want to share people how essential it is to care about climate changes and support the environment and climate changes to create the natural world so that people everywhere will feel its effects.
